Learn how to draw an apple

Equipments

  • 1 Poster colour
  • 2 soft brush
  • drawing book/skecth pad
  • water
  • pencil
  • eraser

Drawing Instructions

  1. sketch a circle that's as wide as you want the apple to be.
  2. Draw a w-shapped line that connects to the bottom of the circle.
  3. Make a m-shapped line that connects to the top of the circle.
  4. Draw over the apple to make the outline bold.
  5. Make 2 parallel lines extend from the top of the apple for a stem.
